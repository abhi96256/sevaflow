## 🧬 2. Complete Feature Breakdown (Module by Module)

### 🔹 CORE MODULES

#### 1. Dashboard
* **Purpose:** The Mission Control Center for the doctor or clinic admin.
* **Features:** 
  * Real-time metrics overview (Today's Patients, Revenue, Pending Appointments).
  * System Status indicators.
  * Clean, widget-based layout utilizing modern glass-card UI for immediate, highly-legible data digestion.

#### 2. Appointments
* **Purpose:** Seamless scheduling and queue management.
* **Features:**
  * Calendar and List views of upcoming patient visits.
  * Integration with WhatsApp: Sends automated confirmation and reminder messages to patients so they don't miss their slot.
  * Easy rescheduling and status tracking (Pending, Completed, Cancelled).

#### 3. Patients (CRM)
* **Purpose:** Core Database of all clinical histories.
* **Features:**
  * Exhaustive patient profiles (Age, Gender, Contact, Baseline Vitals).
  * Historical timeline of all past visits, prescriptions, and lab reports.
  * Advanced Search with real-time dropdown filtering to instantly pull up any patient record.

#### 4. AI Intelligence
* **Purpose:** Predictive analytics and smart insights.
* **Features:**
  * Highlights patient trends.
  * Risk analysis for specific demographics based on the collected clinic data.

#### 5. Prescription (Smart Rx Builder)
* **Purpose:** The most used screen by doctors, engineered for ultra-speed.
* **Features:**
  * **Voice Rx:** Doctors can dictate their notes/diagnosis using the microphone; the system auto-transcribes it (English/Hindi).
  * **Smart Templates:** Save standard protocols (e.g., "Viral Fever", "Acidity"). One click populates the entire medicine list with dosage, timing, and duration.
  * **Lab Test Integration:** Select standard tests (CBC, LFT, etc.) or add custom ones seamlessly.
  * **Digital Signature validation.**
  * **Direct-to-Bill Workflow:** Any medicine or consultation entered here automatically syncs to the Billing module.

---

### 🔹 OPERATIONS MODULES

#### 6. Billing & GST
* **Purpose:** Financial management and tax compliance.
* **Features:**
  * Auto-generation of bills directly out of the Prescription module (eliminates double data entry).
  * Invoice generation with clinic branding.
  * Proper GST breakdowns (CGST/SGST).
  * Receipt tracking (Paid vs Pending).

#### 7. Inventory
* **Purpose:** Managing clinic supplies and consumables.
* **Features:**
  * Track stock levels of syringes, bandages, and clinical assets.
  * Low-stock alerts and usage history.

#### 8. Analytics
* **Purpose:** Clinic performance and revenue tracking.
* **Features:**
  * Beautiful visual charts plotting monthly revenue, most common diagnoses, and patient retention rates.
  * Doctor performance metrics (if polyclinic with multiple staff).

#### 9. AI Innovation Hub (NextGen
* **Purpose:** The Crown Jewel. Where AI meets raw clinical data.
* **Features:**
  * **Neural OCR Decoder:** Upload a photo of an old physical lab report or handwritten prescription (PDF/Image). The AI scans and extracts the diagnosis and medications instantly.
  * **Clinical Health Journey (Chart):** A gorgeous bar-chart timeline showing exactly when the patient visited over the last 7, 30, or all-time days.
  * **Encounter History & Progress:** Analyzes past prescriptions and gives a calculated percentage of "Condition Progress" (e.g., +40% Stable).
  * **General Body / Diagnosis Notes:** A consolidated feed of every single medical note or diagnosis ever written for that patient, sorted chronologically.

#### 10. Pharmacy
* **Purpose:** Dedicated point-of-sale and tracking for in-house medicine dispensaries.
* **Features:**
  * Direct link to the doctor’s prescription. When the doctor saves the Rx, the pharmacy module immediately receives the dispensing queue.
  * Batch number and expiry date tracking.

## ⚙️ 3. The Technical & Aesthetic Edge
1. **Glassmorphism Design:** No more grey, boring screens. SevaFlow uses semi-transparent backgrounds, vibrant accent colors, and smooth micro-animations. It looks premium.
2. **True Automation:** 
   * *Trigger:* Doctor selects "Follow-up in 15 days".
   * *Action:* WhatsApp engine schedules a message for Day 14. 
   * *Action:* Billing portal pre-loads the consultation fee.
3. **No-Lag Architecture:** Built with modern React workflows ensuring instant search results and rapid page routing.
